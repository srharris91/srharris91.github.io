7  PetscBag - PETSc object that manages a collection of user data including parameters.
8  A bag is essentially a C struct with serialization (you can save it and load it from files).
9 
10  Level: beginner
11 
12  Sample Usage:
13 $ typedef struct {
14 $ PetscInt height;
15 $ PetscScalar root;

17 $ } MyParameters;
18 $
19 $ PetscBag bag;
20 $ MyParameters *params;
21 $
22 $ ierr = PetscBagCreate(PETSC_COMM_WORLD,sizeof(MyParameters),&bag);
23 $ ierr = PetscBagGetData(bag,(void **)&params);
24 $ ierr = PetscBagSetName(bag,"MyParameters");
25 $ ierr = PetscBagRegisterInt(bag,&params.height,22,"height","Height of the water tower");
